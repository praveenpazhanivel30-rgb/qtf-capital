Frequently Asked Questions

What is the profit split? +

We offer a 90/10 profit split. You keep 100% of your first $10,000 earned, and 90% of everything after that.

Is there a daily loss limit? +

Unlike other firms, we do not have a hard daily loss limit that fails your account. We use an End-of-Day (EOD) Drawdown model.

When can I request a payout? +

Payouts are processed twice per month. You need a minimum of 5 individual trading days between requests.
